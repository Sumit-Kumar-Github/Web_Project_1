CAMP WITH CARE

Camp with Care is tour-oriented camping facility, which you can travel with your friends and family. The location is situated in the middle of a forest, which makes you feel more connected to the nature. It gives you a very relaxing and positive energy. When you enter the destination, you will get a welcoming drink (coconut water) as compliment. People are being provided with the tents/cottages with bedding items included to stay. Further going through the muddy and sandy roads, you will also encounter a beautiful Beach known as Invisible Beach (Dublagadi Beach). You can reach the destination from Kolkata (250 Km), Balasore railway (32Km) and 17 Km from NH -16 Kolkata Chennai Highway.

Expenses Amount
travelling (cab) ₹1300
lodging ₹ 1300 (per head)
extras ₹ 800 (fooding)
