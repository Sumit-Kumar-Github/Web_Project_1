INVISIBLE BEACH

Dublagadi Beach is one of the less explored beaches which still retains its beauty. It is located 34Km from Balasore. It is a perfect destination for travellers who want to escape the hustle of city and spend a relaxing weekend. It is not a very popular destination, that's why there is not much crowd present, resulting in a clean environment. You will also encounter some sea creatures, living under the sand. The most famous creatures are the red crabs, which will be seen during the nights. Fishermen also go there every day for fishing, if the water level and tides are low. The surrounding has a canopy forest, which shows a beautiful Tyndall effect in the day time. This beach is ideal as a picnic spot. You can also enjoy the campfire during the night time.
