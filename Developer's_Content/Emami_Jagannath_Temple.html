EMAMI JAGANNATH TEMPLE

The Emami Jaganath Temple is a lovely Hindu temple located in Balasore. The temple is 12 Km away from the town of Balasore, spread across a vast area of 12 acres and is surrounded by beautifully landscaped gardens. The temple is pretty new, build in 2009 with sandstones. The temple is adorned with intricate carvings and sculptures. The temple appears as if it has been built on the wheels of a big chariot. The primary deity in the temple is Lord Jaganath, accompanied by Lord Balabhadra and Lord Subhadra. There are also idols of other deities like Lord Ganesh and Lord Hanuman. The temple is managed by a group of priests who perform daily rituals and offer prayers to the deities. 3Km from the temple, there is a lake situated called Blue Lake. It is an artificial lake which has come into being after mining in this place. The reflection of the sun rays on the green water of lake is wonderful. Tourists visit there for picnics and sightseeing.
